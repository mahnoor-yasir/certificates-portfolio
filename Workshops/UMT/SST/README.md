# School of Systems and Technology (SST)

This folder contains certificates earned through workshops, technical training sessions, conferences, and professional development events organized by the **School of Systems and Technology (SST)** at the University of Management and Technology (UMT).

These events provided valuable opportunities to explore emerging technologies, strengthen technical expertise, and gain practical knowledge through hands-on learning experiences. By participating in these programs, I enhanced my understanding of software development, artificial intelligence, game development, and other modern computing technologies while engaging with industry professionals and academic experts.

The certificates in this folder reflect my commitment to continuous learning, technical excellence, and professional growth through active participation in SST's academic and industry-focused initiatives.
