# UMT Office of Professional Services (OPS)

This folder contains certificates earned through workshops, seminars, career development sessions, and professional training programs organized by the **Office of Professional Services (OPS)** at the University of Management and Technology (UMT).

These events focused on developing essential professional skills, including communication, leadership, negotiation, freelancing, career readiness, personal branding, and the practical application of emerging technologies. Through active participation, I gained valuable insights from industry professionals and enhanced both my technical and interpersonal competencies.

The certificates in this folder reflect my commitment to continuous learning, professional development, and preparing for a successful career in the technology industry.
