# IEEE UMT Student Branch

This folder contains certificates earned through workshops, seminars, technical sessions, and professional development events organized by the **IEEE UMT Student Branch** and its affiliated societies, including **IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE)**.

These events provided opportunities to explore emerging technologies, connect with industry professionals, and enhance technical knowledge through interactive learning experiences. The sessions covered topics such as Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, software technologies, innovation, and career development.

The certificates in this folder represent my active participation in IEEE initiatives and my commitment to continuous learning, technical excellence, and professional growth within the engineering and technology community.
