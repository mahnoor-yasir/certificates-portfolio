# Clarivate Workshops

This folder contains certificates earned through workshops, seminars, and research-focused learning programs organized by **Clarivate** and its academic partners.

These programs provided valuable insights into academic research, scholarly communication, research databases, and best practices for conducting high-quality research. Through active participation, I strengthened my understanding of literature discovery, citation analysis, research evaluation, and the effective use of research tools to support academic and professional work.

The certificates in this folder reflect my commitment to continuous learning, research excellence, and the development of essential skills for academic and professional success.
