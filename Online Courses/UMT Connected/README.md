# UMT Connected

This folder contains certificates earned through professional and academic courses completed under **UMT Connected**, an initiative of the University of Management and Technology (UMT).

The courses offered through UMT Connected are designed to strengthen students' academic, professional, and communication skills by providing structured learning experiences aligned with industry and workplace requirements. Through these programs, I have enhanced my English communication, career readiness, and personal development while preparing for academic and professional success.

The certificates in this folder reflect my commitment to continuous learning, professional growth, and acquiring practical skills through quality educational programs offered by UMT Connected.
