# Tech Fiesta 2026

This folder contains certificates earned through competitions held during **Tech Fiesta 2026**, a technical event organized by the **UMT Robotics and Automation Society** in collaboration with the **Department of Electrical Engineering** and the **School of Engineering** at the University of Management and Technology (UMT).

Tech Fiesta 2026 provided participants with opportunities to engage in competitive activities, demonstrate their skills, and collaborate with fellow students in a dynamic learning environment. Through participation in these competitions, I enhanced my teamwork, problem-solving, communication, and decision-making abilities while gaining valuable experience beyond the classroom.

The certificates in this folder reflect my enthusiasm for participating in technical competitions, embracing new challenges, and continuously developing both technical and interpersonal skills.
