# Talent Quest Fest 2.0

This folder contains certificates earned through competitions held during **Talent Quest Fest 2.0**, organized by the **School of Systems and Technology (SST)** at the University of Management and Technology (UMT).

Talent Quest Fest 2.0 provided an engaging platform for students to showcase their skills, compete in diverse academic and extracurricular activities, and foster teamwork, creativity, and healthy competition. Through active participation in these events, I enhanced my problem-solving abilities, communication skills, confidence, and overall personal development.

The certificates in this folder reflect my enthusiasm for participating in competitive events, embracing new challenges, and continuously developing both technical and non-technical skills.
